About Us

Corporate Summary

Petrware is an up-and-coming tech-focused online retailer in the United States, exclusively serving the great lower 48 states of America. Founded in 2020 during the Great Global COVID-19 Pandemic, the company currently offers a high-end selection of the latest and most popular consumer computer hardware products. Petrware aims to become one of the best online shopping destinations focused on achieving industry-leading customer service ratings. We are headquartered at the University of California, Irvine campus with planned distribution centers located throughout the nation.
